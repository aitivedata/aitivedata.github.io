Aitive Data - Brand Assets
==========================

Files:
  logo-light-bg.svg  - Full logo for light/white backgrounds (invoices, contracts, print)
  logo-dark-bg.svg   - Full logo for dark backgrounds (website, presentations)
  logo-icon.svg      - Icon only, square format (social media profiles, favicon)

Colors:
  Accent (Data):     #D64B6A
  Accent Light:      #E06B86
  Text Dark:         #1E1E23
  Text Light:        #E8E6E3
  Background Dark:   #0E0F11
  Surface:           #161719

Font:
  Libre Baskerville (Bold 700) - Google Fonts
  Fallback: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif

Usage:
  - For PNG export: open SVG in browser or Figma, export at 2x or 3x
  - For print: use SVG directly or export as high-res PNG (300 DPI)
  - sevDesk: export logo-light-bg.svg as PNG
