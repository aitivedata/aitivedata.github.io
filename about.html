About Us

// The people behind Aitive Data

Aitive Data was founded by Bernhard and Victoria Glück with a clear mission: help businesses turn their data into real value. Based in Klosterneuburg near Vienna, we combine deep technical expertise with a hands-on, results-driven approach.

Where others see complexity, we see opportunity. Whether it's building a data warehouse from scratch, integrating AI into existing workflows, or making sense of scattered data — we deliver solutions that work.

Engineering & AI

Bernhard Glück

Co-Founder, Cooperation Partner & Advisory

With over 25 years in software engineering, Bernhard brings deep expertise in AI systems, software architecture, and performance-critical applications. He has led teams of hundreds of developers and delivered solutions for companies like Mercedes-Benz, ProSiebenSat.1, BCG, and Entain.

As cooperation partner and technical advisor, Bernhard bridges Aitive Data with Aitive Technologies GmbH — bringing enterprise-grade AI and engineering capabilities to every project.
